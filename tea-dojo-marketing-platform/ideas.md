## Selected Design: Warm Tech Naturalism

I'm selecting **Warm Tech Naturalism** for this project because it best bridges Tea Dojo's traditional bubble tea culture with the innovative AI technology of the marketing platform. The warm, earthy palette creates an inviting atmosphere while maintaining professionalism. The organic shapes and natural textures connect to tea's origins, while the clean UI elements communicate technological capability.

### Implementation Notes:

**Colors (OKLCH for Tailwind 4):**
- Background: oklch(0.99 0.01 85) - warm cream
- Foreground: oklch(0.25 0.04 160) - deep forest green
- Primary: oklch(0.35 0.08 160) - forest green
- Accent: oklch(0.60 0.12 45) - terracotta
- Gold: oklch(0.75 0.08 75) - soft gold

**Fonts:**
- Fraunces (Google Fonts) - Display
- Source Sans 3 (Google Fonts) - Body

## 1.0 Introduction

This document outlines a proposal for the design and development of a comprehensive, AI-powered marketing platform for Tea Dojo Singapore. The platform is designed to streamline marketing workflows, enhance content creation, and provide data-driven insights to boost brand engagement and sales.

## 2.0 Executive Summary

This project aims to build a centralized platform that leverages artificial intelligence to automate and optimize Tea Dojoâ€™s marketing activities. Key features include an AI Copywriting Assistant, AI Visual and Video Script Generation, a Content Calendar with one-click social media posting, and a Performance Analytics dashboard. The platform will empower the Tea Dojo marketing team to create high-quality, on-brand content at scale, while tracking performance to refine future strategies.

## 3.0 Project Goals and Objectives

*   **Goal 1:** To increase marketing efficiency by automating content creation and distribution.
    *   **Objective:** Reduce time spent on creating social media posts by 50%.
    *   **Objective:** Automate the scheduling and posting of content across all major social media channels.
*   **Goal 2:** To enhance brand consistency and engagement across all marketing channels.
    *   **Objective:** Ensure all generated content aligns with Tea Dojo's brand voice and visual style.
    *   **Objective:** Increase social media engagement rates by 25% within the first six months of platform use.
*   **Goal 3:** To provide data-driven insights for strategic decision-making.
    *   **Objective:** Implement a real-time analytics dashboard to track key performance indicators (KPIs).
    *   **Objective:** Generate monthly reports with actionable insights and recommendations.

## 4.0 Scope of Work

### 4.1 Phase 1: Core Platform Development (MVP)

*   **AI Content Generation Suite:**
    *   AI Copywriting Assistant (for Instagram, Facebook, TikTok)
    *   Brand Voice & Style Customization
*   **Content Strategy & Management:**
    *   Content Calendar with manual scheduling
*   **Analytics & Customization:**
    *   Basic Performance Analytics Dashboard (tracking likes, comments, shares)

### 4.2 Phase 2: Advanced Features & Integrations

*   **AI Content Generation Suite:**
    *   AI Visual Generation (for images and promotional materials)
    *   AI Video Script Generation
    *   AI Event & Experiential Marketing Ideas
*   **Content Strategy & Management:**
    *   One-Click Posting (integration with Facebook, Instagram, TikTok APIs)
    *   Promotional Event Forecasting in Content Calendar
*   **Analytics & Customization:**
    *   Advanced Analytics (reach, conversion, trend analysis)

### 4.3 Phase 3: Merchandise and Design

*   **AI Visual Generation:**
    *   Merchandise and Packaging Design (cups, tote bags, tumblers)

## 5.0 Proposed Technical Architecture

*   **Frontend:** React (Vite), TypeScript, TailwindCSS
*   **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js, PostgreSQL, Drizzle/Prisma
*   **AI Integrations:** OpenAI API (GPT-4), DALL-E 3 / Midjourney API
*   **Deployment:** Vercel/Netlify (Frontend), AWS/Google Cloud (Backend)
